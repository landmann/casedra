## 0. The Madrid asymmetry

We start with an unfair advantage most startups don't have: **three friends already working in Madrid real estate.** This is the oxygen for week one. Every plan below is built on activating them ruthlessly.

- 3 friends × ~20 agent colleagues each = a ~60-agent warm intro network before we write a line of cold outreach.
- Madrid is Spain's hottest market: ~150k residential transactions/year, ~6,000 agencies, a tight proptech scene, and the geographic center for press and investors.
- Everyone in Spanish real estate passes through SIMA (Salón Inmobiliario de Madrid) in May/October. That's our trade-show arena.
- Madrid barrios are compact and walkable: Chamberí, Salamanca, Retiro, Chamartín. Door-to-door field sales is physically possible in a way it isn't in LA or London.

**Rule: until we have €50k MRR, we don't spend a single hour selling outside Madrid.**

## 1. Week 1–2 (Apr 17 → May 1, 2026): The Three-Friend Launch

**Goal:** first paying customer within 10 days of product being functional. Hit gate **G0 (€300 MRR / 5 paying agencies) by May 1, 2026.**

### Actions

- **Day 1–3 (product side):** media studio staging + copy generation live in production, even if only two agencies can see it. Stripe checkout functional.
- **Day 1 (GTM side):** in-person coffee with each of the three friend-agents. They see a live demo on their own listings. You ask two things:
  - "Will you pay €79/mo for this as an early customer?"
  - "Which 3 colleagues in your office will pay for this if I show them the same demo?"
- **Day 4–7:** ship renders for real listings from all three friends' agencies. Screenshot the before/after. These are the marketing assets for the next 3 months.
- **Day 8–10:** host a casual in-office demo breakfast at each of the three agencies (pan tumaca and café). 5–15 agents in each room. Close 2–3 subscriptions per visit.
- **Day 10–14:** record a 90-second product video with one of the friends as the on-camera agent. Post to their LinkedIn and Instagram. Our first inbound.

### Targets (by 2026-05-01, gate G0)
- 5+ paying agencies
- €300+ MRR
- 10 before/after case-study screenshots banked
- First 3 testimonial videos recorded
- 2+ agencies using Responde (not just Studio)

### The pitch (memorize it)

> "You know that flat in Tetuán you've had on the market for 3 months? The owner's kid has just cleaned the whole thing and it still looks like shit in photos. Give me that listing. Three minutes. Watch what happens."

Then you stage it live. Price after wow, never before.

## 2. Month 1–3 (May 17 → Jul 17, 2026): Madrid Agency Invasion

**Goal:** gate **G2 (€15k MRR / 120 paying agencies) by Jul 17, 2026.** Intermediate checkpoint: gate **G1 (€2k MRR / 20 agencies) by May 17, 2026.**

### Three parallel motions

#### Motion A: Field sales (the primary engine)

- **Walk the barrios.** Madrid has ~6,000 agencies. A 2-person sales team can visit 15 offices per day. That's 300/month per rep.
- **Geo-target:** Chamberí, Salamanca, Chamartín, Retiro, Argüelles, Tetuán first (higher avg ticket = higher willingness to pay).
- **The pitch:** "We help your worst listing sell faster. Got 3 minutes?" Live demo on their own Idealista listing. Close on the spot or email within 24h.
- **Team:** one founder does field sales full-time in months 1–3. Hire a first BDR at month 2 (a former agent ideally — look at LinkedIn for agents who've recently left their agency, €30–40k + commission).

#### Motion B: Referral program

- **1 month free for each paying referral.** Stackable — refer 12 = a year free.
- **Agent leaderboards** visible in-app. Gamify. Every Madrid agent knows every other Madrid agent.
- **Ambassador tier:** top-3 referrers per quarter get a dinner with the founders and a custom "Casablanca Partner" badge on their microsite.

#### Motion C: Content + social

- **Case-study Instagram account** (@casablanca.inmobiliaria): daily before/after. No text — the images do the work.
- **TikTok for agents:** 60-second demos, Spanish, in an agent's voice. Target: 10 videos/week, one founder does this.
- **LinkedIn** (Spanish): founders post weekly. Pattern: agency case study → revenue/time saved → one specific product feature.
- **YouTube long-form:** 1 video/week, 10–15 min, "Cómo presentar un piso en Idealista para que se venda en 14 días". SEO gold.

### Events
- Sponsor 1–2 local agent meetups per month (API Madrid, Colegio de Agentes de la Propiedad Inmobiliaria). €500–1,500 each = exposure to 50+ agents.
- Host **Casablanca Breakfast** monthly: 20 agents, 1 founder, a real live demo session. Free croissants. Close 5 accounts.

### Do not do yet
- Google Ads (wasted spend for SMB real estate where Google intent is low).
- Big conferences (SIMA is in month 5+).
- International.
- White-label partnerships (dilutes brand; wait until we're a brand).

### Targets (by 2026-07-17, gate G2)
- 120 paying agencies
- €15k MRR
- 35+ case-study assets
- 50+ inbound agent requests
- 2 first agency-tier deals (€349+/mo)
- 10+ Inmovilla migrations completed (framing: "escape Idealista's walled garden")

**Goal:** €200M ARR by Apr 17, 2031. Live in 4–6 European markets. **€1B valuation event no later than 2031-04-17.**

**Dated milestones inside this window:**
- **2029-04-17:** €100M ARR (€8.4M MRR, 20,000 agencies). Series B territory.

- **2031-04-17:** €200M ARR (€16.7M MRR, 35,000 agencies). **€1B valuation event** (Series C, IPO, or strategic exit).

### France — year 3
- Hardest market: ~130k agents, high ARPU, complex regulation (loi Hoguet, mandate exclusivity, DPE energy-label reform), SeLoger/Bien'ici/LeBonCoin fragmented portal landscape.
- Requires ~€5M of localized investment.
- Launches with enterprise-first motion (targeting Century 21 France, ORPI, Laforêt franchises).

### Germany — year 4
- Biggest EU market, ImmoScout24 dominant, enterprise-heavy, slower to adopt.
- Enters via acquisition of a local CRM (target: onOffice's weaker competitors) + product migration.

### Strategic moves year 3+
- **Acquire Inmovilla or Witei** for their customer base. Estimated cost: €20–50M. Migrate users to Casablanca in 6 months. Kills 30% of Spanish competitive pressure instantly.
- **Launch consumer portal** (SearchCasa) in cities where we have 25%+ listing supply. Marketing-as-listings: every agency is a free distribution channel.
- **Casablanca Capital:** explore direct mortgage origination via a BaaS partner. Requires €100M+ balance sheet; Series C financing.

### Series B, then C
- **Series B:** €40–70M at €300–500M post, year 3. At €15M+ ARR with transaction rails >25% of revenue.
- **Series C (optional):** €100M+ at €1B+ post, year 4–5. This is the €1B valuation event. Investors: Tiger-class growth funds, SoftBank-adjacent, potentially strategic (Idealista's backers EQT, or Schibsted).
- **Alternative exit:** strategic acquisition by Idealista/EQT, Immobiliare.it's owner, or a global proptech consolidator at this stage.

## 7. Pricing evolution

| Phase | SKU | Monthly | What you get |
|---|---|---|---|
| 0 | Lite | €29 | 10 renders/mo |
| 0 | Pro | €79 | 50 renders + copy + floor plans |
| 0 | Agencia | €249 | 3 seats, 300 renders, priority |
| 1 | Pro (upgraded) | €99 | + listing cockpit, multi-portal export |
| 1 | Agencia (upgraded) | €399 | + WhatsApp inbox, seller reports |
| 2 | Starter | €99/seat | Full agent OS, 1 seat |
| 2 | Agencia | €299 | 5 seats, full OS |
| 2 | Agencia Pro | €149/seat | 6+ seats, white-label microsite |
| 3+ | Enterprise | custom | Franchise/chain deals, €30–150k/year |
| 3+ | Transaction rails | take-rate | 5–50bps on mortgage/escrow/notary |

**Pricing principles:**
- **Always anchor to ROI.** "This costs less than one hour of your time / one professional photo session."
- **Annual prepay: 20% off.** Cash flow lifesaver in year 1.
- **Never discount SaaS at the point of sale.** If they won't pay list, they won't renew.
- **Enterprise discounts only with multi-year prepay.**
- **Grandfather early customers forever.** First 100 paying agents never get a price raise. Loyalty compounds.

## 8. Unit economics (GTM perspective)

- **CAC (field sales):** €180 per agent (1 BDR cost + tools amortized)

- **Payback:** 1.5 months blended

- **Net revenue retention:** target 115% by month 12

- **Blended ARPU:** €400/mo (SaaS + take-rate)
- **NRR:** 140%
- **Payback:** <1 month
- **Gross margin:** 72% (lower because of take-rate partner splits, but much higher absolute margin per customer)

## 10. Partnership playbook

### Phase 1 partnerships (month 3–9)
- **Fal.ai co-marketing** — "built on Fal" case study. They promote us to proptech; we give them volume.
- **Idealista (yes, really)** — offer Casablanca as a free tool to their XML-feed agencies. Positioning: "we make your Idealista listings better; pay Idealista more promotion, sell faster." Idealista's economics improve. Ours grow 10x via access to their agencies.
- **API (Agentes de la Propiedad Inmobiliaria)** — the certification body. Sponsor their education program, offer Casablanca at 50% off to certified agents. Signals legitimacy.
- **Real estate schools** — Inmoacademia, Urbaniza. Teach our product in their courses. Every new agent graduates into Casablanca.

### Phase 2 partnerships (month 9–18)
- **Mortgage brokers** — iAhorro, TrioTeca, Idealista Hipotecas. Embed their flow; revshare 50/50 on originated loans.
- **Notarías** — Ancert (the notary-network software vendor). API access to the notary booking system.
- **Energy certificate providers** — CertiCalia and similar. Embed the quote flow.
- **Moving + utilities** — Mudanzas.com, Iberdrola, Naturgy. Referral revenue post-closing.

### Phase 3 partnerships (year 2+)
- **Banks** (BBVA, Sabadell, Santander) for direct mortgage origination rails. BaaS-style deals.
- **Insurers** (Mapfre, Mutua, Línea Directa) for home insurance at closing.
- **Registros** (Colegio de Registradores) for nota simple automation.

## 11. Defensive plays (when competitors wake up)

### If Idealista launches competing media tools
- Public response: "We syndicate to Idealista. We're their partner, not their competitor."
- Private response: double down on CRM + transaction. Idealista is slow; by the time they ship, we own the workflow layer.
- Pricing response: match or undercut on media; upsell on CRM.

### If Inmovilla/Witei launches AI features
- Press release highlighting our feature lead (we'll be 12+ months ahead).
- Migration tool: "Import your Inmovilla/Witei data in one click." Offer 3 months free for switchers.
- Target: convert 20% of their base within 18 months of their AI launch.

### If a VC-backed competitor enters Spain
- Out-ship them weekly. Our moat is speed.
- Lock in enterprise deals first with 2-year contracts.
- Lean on our three-friend mafia: every agent we've helped becomes a reference.

### If a US proptech (Compass, Opendoor, ShowingTime) enters
- Unlikely — regulation per country is their blocker, not product.
- If they do: partnership first (white-label Casablanca), compete second.

## 15. The one thing we must not forget

The ceiling on this company is not the product. The product is almost easy now.

The ceiling is whether we can build enough trust with 1,000 Madrid agents for them to rebuild their daily work around us before Idealista wakes up.

That trust is built in person, one coffee at a time, for the first 12 months. Founders are in field. Always. No exceptions.

Everything in this document serves that one idea.
